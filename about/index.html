About

Sep 28, 2019
1 min read

Hi! I’m Ruben. I am interested in building products, software, and management.

I currently work as a team lead and software engineer at Spacemaker where we try to find better ways to design our cities.

Previously I have co-founded two companies: A traditional startup, Jobbr, a platform where you could undertake and post smaller jobs, and Korde, a consulting company that helped startups and established companies with business and software development.

I have a masters degree in computer science with a specialization in geoinformatics from the Norwegian university of science and technology. In my thesis, my partner and I tried to create maps from satellite photos using convolutional neural networks.
